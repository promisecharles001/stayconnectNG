# ABOUT STAYCONNECT NG

StayConnectNG is a technology-driven accommodation marketplace designed to connect travelers and guests with verified vacant rooms in hotels, serviced apartments, and privately owned homes across Nigeria and beyond.

The platform bridges the gap between property owners and guests by enabling location-based discovery of nearby accommodation, direct communication with hosts, and navigation to properties using integrated mapping technology. StayConnectNG empowers private homeowners, small-scale operators, and hospitality providers to monetize unused rooms while giving travelers flexible, affordable, and accessible lodging options.

By combining geolocation, host verification, peer ratings, and a streamlined booking workflow, StayConnectNG aims to digitize Nigeria's fragmented short-stay accommodation market and build a trusted peer-to-peer lodging ecosystem.

StayConnectNG operates as a neutral digital marketplace, facilitating connections between independent hosts and guests while promoting transparency, accountability, and convenience through technology.
