## Full Description (4000 chars max)
StayConnectNG is Nigeria's trusted accommodation marketplace connecting travelers with verified hosts offering vacant rooms, serviced apartments, and homes.

**FOR GUESTS:**
🔍 Discover nearby accommodations using our map-based search
🏠 Browse verified listings with photos, amenities & reviews
💬 Chat directly with hosts before booking
📍 Get turn-by-turn navigation to your stay
📅 Book instantly or request dates
⭐ Rate and review your experience

**FOR HOSTS:**
🏡 List your property in minutes
📊 Manage bookings & availability
💰 Earn income from unused rooms
🛡️ Verified host badge builds trust
📱 Receive booking notifications

**WHY STAYCONNECT NG?**
✅ Verified hosts & properties
✅ No hidden fees - transparent pricing
✅ Secure communication
✅ Location-based discovery
✅ Direct host-guest connection
✅ Support for Nigerian payment methods

Whether you're a traveler looking for affordable lodging or a property owner wanting to monetize your space, StayConnectNG makes it simple, safe, and convenient.

Download now and find your perfect stay!
